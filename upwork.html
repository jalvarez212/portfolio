The brief

Partnered with Upwork during a phase of rapid growth—supporting the business through its IPO and into the post-IPO period—while managing and scaling paid search across the US and Europe. Focused on connecting local business owners with freelancers efficiently, ensuring campaigns could scale without sacrificing cost discipline or funnel quality.

Led a data-driven testing program across bidding strategies, ad copy, and landing experiences that drove down acquisition costs and unlocked new pools of high-intent traffic, helping Upwork balance aggressive growth targets with long-term efficiency.

About Upwork

The challenge

Aggressive growth targets across multiple regions with varying competitive dynamics required a scalable structure and precise geo/keyword controls—without sacrificing efficiency. Upwork was running large-scale global campaigns in the US and Europe across Google Search, Bing, and programmatic media, making coordination and optimization critical to performance.

The solution

Re-architected campaigns by market and intent tiers, introduced geo-bid modifiers, and rolled out systematic A/B testing for copy and LPs. Conducted deep keyword analysis to identify and filter out search terms driving up CAC, applying negative keywords to block low-intent traffic. Optimized query mapping and bidding to shift spend to converting terms. Result: a 30% reduction in customer acquisition cost on Google Search and Bing while increasing qualified lead volume.
