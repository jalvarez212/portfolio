The brief

Support the Salesforce Partners team on both sides of the ecosystem—recruit new partners and acquire new customers for partners—through integrated search and social campaigns across regions.

The central insight driving campaign planning was that existing Salesforce customers had a higher propensity to become successful Salesforce partners. This meant our strategy needed to identify and prioritize those customers most likely to transition from product adoption to partner enablement.

Build a measurement framework aligning single- and multi-touch attribution to business goals to enable confident budget allocation and renewals.

The challenge

Multiple objectives (partner recruitment and customer acquisition), diverse tech stacks, and long sales cycles required clean tracking, CRM alignment, and channel orchestration without inflating CPLs.

The solution

We operationalized the insight that Salesforce customers made stronger partner candidates by segmenting campaign audiences into two tracks: (1) net-new prospects, and (2) existing Salesforce customers with demonstrated adoption. Messaging, creative, and bidding strategies were tailored accordingly—leveraging case studies and ecosystem success stories for existing customers, while using education-first content for net-new prospects.

Designed an end-to-end measurement stack (UTM/param standards, pixel/CRM integrations), deployed single- and multi-touch models, and operationalized dashboards for pipeline visibility. Executed LinkedIn, Google, and Meta campaigns with audience layering (ABM lists, intent data), creative testing, and budget pacing tied to down-funnel signals. This insight-led approach not only improved efficiency but also contributed to consistent scope renewals over two years.
