The brief

Hello Solar set out to build a customer base around clean energy products and content, with the dual goal of driving e-commerce sales and creating an engaged community advocating for sustainable living.

The team needed a strategy that combined paid channels, organic content, and email to fuel growth while providing real-time visibility into performance.

The challenge

Hello Solar needed to acquire new customers and engage existing ones on a startup budget. The challenge was to stand out in the crowded renewable energy space while building the infrastructure to track and optimize campaigns across multiple channels.

The solution

I designed and executed cross-channel campaigns across Google Ads, Bing Ads, Instagram, and email. I launched an e-commerce website with landing pages, product feeds, and real-time measurement systems. By implementing growth loops and viral strategies, we grew the newsletter list from 0 to 3,000 subscribers. This combination of acquisition + retention marketing allowed Hello Solar to scale sustainably while deepening customer relationships.
