The brief

Plan and execute high-volume seasonal campaigns across Facebook, Google Display, YouTube, and Instagram to grow new customer acquisition and drive Q4 revenue.

Coordinate with account management on forecasting and pacing to align spend with weekly sales targets and inventory constraints.

See Media Coverage

The challenge

Peak-season competition and short windows for conversion required precise audience segmentation, creative fit by funnel stage, and rapid optimization to maintain ROI.

The solution

Built multi-touch targeting (prospecting → consideration → retargeting) with creative variants per stage, synchronized product feeds, and real-time reporting. Managed six-figure budgets and reallocated spend based on contribution margin. Result: a 35% year-over-year ROI increase on holiday campaigns.
