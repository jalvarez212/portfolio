The brief

Spanish bank ABANCA tapped our team to develop a custom web experience using 3D/VR rendering in the browser. As a special holiday gift to select customers, ABANCA wanted a Christmas-themed site where users could explore the night sky and discover constellation messages.

Beyond project management and building the website, I supported ABANCA in marketing the campaign to key internal and external stakeholders—ensuring alignment, engagement, and successful adoption of the experience.

View Github

The challenge

A critical aspect of this project was developing a dynamic rendering system that could import JSON data and draw constellation messages in the scene—164 in total. The system needed to be efficient and lightweight to perform across devices.

Beyond project management and building the website, I supported ABANCA in marketing the campaign to key internal and external stakeholders—communicating its value through a highly targeted social campaign that drove awareness and reinforced the brand’s innovative positioning.

The solution

We used the open source framework Three.js to build a 3D scene capable of rendering constellations dynamically. Our lightweight particle animation system imported JSON coordinates and illuminated constellations in the sky when triggered by user interactions. Alongside the build, I partnered with ABANCA’s team to communicate the campaign’s value to stakeholders through a highly targeted social campaign, amplifying reach and securing buy-in.
