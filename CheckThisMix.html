Hip Hop Playlist - Summer 2018

It's been a scorching summer ladies and gents. So much new music out there, it's hard to keep up with all of it. Literally every major artist has dropped a project this year. Drake, Pusha-T, Nas, Kanye West, Jay-Z, Beyonce, Rich Da Kid, Travis Scott, Migos, A$AP Rocky, J Cole. I mean the list goes on for days. And that’s just one genre. Electronic music is a whole other monster.

I put together some of my favorite tracks from projects that have dropped this year. It’s a long list. Give it a listen, let it rock, and let me know what ya think. I might post an electronic music playlist soon. idk, don’t hold it against me if I don’t.
