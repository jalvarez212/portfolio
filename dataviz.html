Renewable Energy Policy & Lithium Battery Production Data Visualization



I created an interactive map outlining all the cities in the US that have pledged to 100% renewable energy. The map marked each city's total population along with the corresponding year governments pledged to accomplish by. The visualization was picked up by several media publications including Cleantechnica. I developed this map as a way to use my skill set to advocate for sustainable and environmental initiatives. Originally published on, Hello Solar.

Since first publishing this story I have continued to build upon this sustainable policy theme. I went on to develop data visualizations outlining lithium production in South America. Batteries are already a huge part of our lives and will be critical in the progress towards more renewable forms of energy. This story was also originally published on Hello Solar.

The juxtaposition of these two data sets begins to highlight potential correlations between both stories.
