The brief

Kiehl's was launching two new facial masque lines, Cilantro & Orange Extract and Turmeric & Cranberry Extract, with Facebook as a key growth channel. The brand also needed strong holiday campaigns to expand digital reach and capture seasonal demand.

I managed six-figure paid media budgets across social channels, driving a 35% year-over-year ROI increase through multi-touch targeting strategies. I partnered closely with account management to forecast performance metrics and optimize ad spend efficiency—aligning media strategies with client goals and maximizing return on investment.

Launch project

The challenge

Working within an 8-week product launch window and fast-moving holiday calendar, we needed to expand Kiehl's digital reach while delivering ROI-positive results and efficient pacing of large media budgets.

The solution

I developed and executed cross-channel media plans, optimized budget allocation, and layered targeting strategies to expand Kiehl’s audience reach. Forecasting and measurement frameworks ensured spend efficiency and long-term ROI growth, while retargeting drove early sales momentum.
