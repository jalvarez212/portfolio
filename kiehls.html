The brief

Kiehl's was launching two new facial masque lines, Cilantro & Orange extract and Turmeric & Cranberry extract.

Kiehl's wanted to launch a campaign announcing the new masques lines on Facebook. Facebook being one of their fastest growing marketing channels.

Launch project

The challenge

Working with a 8 week timeline, 4 weeks to develop the campaign and 4 weeks to launch it. We needed to expand Kiehl's digital reach while returning a positive ROI on the campaign.

The solution

I was part of a cross-disciplinary team that developed, planned, and executed a campaign that would allow Kiehl's to reach new audiences with the new product launch and drive early sales through retargeting strategies.
