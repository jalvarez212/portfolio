The Dominican American Experience

Back in 2014, I gave a talk about my Dominican heritage to a group of co-workers. I pride myself on my diverse network of friends and the diversity within my own heritage. The different cultures that make up the American experience share more in common than we might realize. By sharing this post about my own experience as a Dominican American, I hope to begin talking more about what makes us similar.
