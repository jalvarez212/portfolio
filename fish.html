The brief

The Fish Company, founded in 2011 by the O’Hanlon family. They provided customers a globally sourced inventory of fresh and frozen seafood in the South Florida area. The Fish Company worked with restaurants, hotels, and small cafés to offer them high quality seafood. The team prided itself on its quality and customer service.

Launch project

The challenge

The Miami based company, wanted to position themselves to offer their excellent seafood and service to more customers in the area. The team wanted to explore opportunities with digital marketing to help bring in new business.

The solution

We built the companies search & social game plan from the ground up. This involved setting up proper analytics and developing custom landing pages designed for new audiences. We implemented robust search & social tactics to reach business owners and entrepreneurs who were looking for supplies of fresh fish.
